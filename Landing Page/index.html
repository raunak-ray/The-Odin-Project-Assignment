Thor – The God of Thunder

From the skies of Asgard to the battlefields of Midgard, Thor wields his mighty power with courage and honor. Protector of realms, son of Odin, and bearer of lightning — he stands as the guardian of gods and men alike.

Who is Thor?

Thor Odinson is the Norse God of Thunder, one of the most powerful beings in the Nine Realms. Known for his unmatched strength and indomitable spirit, he is a warrior who defends Asgard and Earth from forces of chaos. With his hammer in hand, he channels the storm itself — a symbol of both fury and protection. His heart, though forged in battle, beats for justice, honor, and those he loves.

Mjolnir

Forged in the heart of a dying star, Mjolnir is Thor’s legendary hammer — a weapon of immense power that grants him command over lightning and the ability to soar through the skies. Only those deemed worthy can lift it.

Stormbreaker

Born from the forge of Nidavellir, Stormbreaker is an axe-hammer hybrid capable of summoning the Bifröst and channeling the full fury of the storm. It symbolizes Thor’s rebirth and unshakable resolve.

Gungnir

The spear of Odin and a symbol of Asgardian royalty, Gungnir represents divine authority and unmatched precision. Though not Thor’s main weapon, it reminds him of his lineage and the power of leadership.
