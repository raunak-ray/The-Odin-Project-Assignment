Masala Maggi

A spicy and flavorful twist on regular Maggi noodles, cooked with onions, tomatoes, and Indian spices for a homely taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 cup water

Method:

  1. Heat oil and fry onion and tomato for 2 minutes.
  2. Add turmeric and chili powder. Mix well.
  3. Add water and Maggi noodles with tastemaker.
