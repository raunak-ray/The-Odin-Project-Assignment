Paneer Sandwich

A simple and healthy sandwich filled with spiced paneer and veggies, ideal for breakfast or a light snack.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bread slices
  • 100g paneer (crumbled)
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 capsicum (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for toasting

Method:

  1. Mix paneer, onion, capsicum, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread the mixture on bread slices.
  3. Cover with another slice and toast with butter until golden.
  4. Cut and serve with ketchup.
