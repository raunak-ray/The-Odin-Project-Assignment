Bananna Smoothie

A creamy and refreshing drink made with banana, milk, and honey â€” a perfect energy booster for any time of day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 ice cubes

Method:

  1. Add banana, milk, honey, and ice to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
