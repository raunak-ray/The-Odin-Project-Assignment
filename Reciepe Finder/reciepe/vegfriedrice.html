Veg Fried Rice

A quick and tasty rice dish made with colorful vegetables and soy sauce. Perfect for lunch or dinner with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small carrot (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté all vegetables for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Add cooked rice and soy sauce.
  3. Mix well and cook for 2–3 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Add salt and mix again. Serve hot.
