Aloo Paratha

A soft and crispy stuffed flatbread filled with spicy mashed potatoes, served best with curd or pickle.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt and chili powder to taste
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Method:

  1. Knead dough with wheat flour and water.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with salt, chili, and cumin powder.
  3. Roll dough, stuff potato mix, and roll again.
  4. Cook on a pan with ghee until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
